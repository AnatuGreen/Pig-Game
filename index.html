How to play Pig Game üòç

1. The game is between two people.
2. Player 1 always starts
3. Click the "Roll Dice" button once to generate a random number from 1 to 6
4. If you get 1, you score zero for that roll and it is player 2's turn. The switch is automatic
5. If you get anything other than 1 you can "Hold" it and your total score will increase.
6. You can decide to roll multiple times to grow your "Current" score before you "Hold". But note that while you are doing that, you may roll 1 which will cancel the current score you have to zero and switch to your opponent.
7. The first player to reach 100 or above wins and the game will stop, you can click "New Game" to restart game.
8. Goodluck!
